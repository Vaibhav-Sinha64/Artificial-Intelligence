What is Artificial Intelligence ?

Artificial intelligence (AI) is the branch of computer science that builds systems able to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence, such as recognizing speech, understanding language, and making decisions.

Why to use AI ?

5

Efficiency: Automates repetitive tasks, reducing time and labor costs.
Accuracy: Performs complex analysis with fewer errors than humans, especially at scale.
Insights: Discovers patterns in large datasets that humans would miss, enabling better decision-making.
24/7 Availability: Systems work continuously without fatigue, improving customer service and productivity.
Innovation: Enables new capabilities in healthcare, finance, transportation, and creative fields.
